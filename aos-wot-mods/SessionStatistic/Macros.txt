----------- Messages after battle ------------
macros in posts
{{result}} - macro expands depending on the outcome of the battle to the text defined in a "isWin", "isDraw", "isDefeat"
{{pure}} - label . in its place later, when data are available for repairs , etc. , will be written that is defined in line purecredits
{{vehicle}} - the name of art
{{credits}} - earned ( in The message of remnote - spent ) money
{{xp}} - Experience per Battle to the coefficients and all deductions
{{dailyXPFactor}} - multiplier experience
{{pure?redits}} - net income for the fight
{{tmenXP}} - experience tankers
{{arenaName}} - title card
{{createdAt}} - a time when the fight was played
{{achieves}} - for all the achievements of the battle in one line
{{xpPenalty}} - fine for damage to the allies
{{creditsContributionIn}} paying you for the damage done to your allies
{{creditsContributionOut}} - fine you for damage caused by the Allies
{{RepairCost}} - repair costs
{{AmmoCost}} - for replenishment costs BC
{{EqipmentCost}} - the cost of equipment
{{quests}} - Award for the performing combat tasks
